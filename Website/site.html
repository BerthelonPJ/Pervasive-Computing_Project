Controlling the sensors with your hand

Your hands control the smart devices

Smart houses, personal assistants and connected devices are more and more part of our daily environnement. We proposed an alternative to voice controlled houses, by offering the ability to the user to view its devices through an holographic view, using mixed reality and hand gestures to control them.

FAQ

How can I get access to smart object which use Z-Wave network ? You need Z-Wave bridge like Z-Stick gen 5 provided by Aeotec and a library to communicate. OpenZwave creates free software library that you can use to manipulate devices on Z-Wave network.

How can I configure Hololens project ? The best way is just to follow the official documentation : https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/mixed-reality/install-the-tools.
